Make things better

Alfred is a new kind of company. We're not in a specific industry or space. Instead, teams with different backgrounds and skills choose a problem to solve. We learn by experimenting, until we find a solution that consider both people and planet. We launch the solution and the profit helps us continue to solve problems.

Our goal is to make things a lot better. We're not there yet and we might make mistakes on the way, but if we do, we'll try our best to fix them.

We're interested in renewable energy, sustainable transportation, clean water & food creation, learning, health and space exploration.
